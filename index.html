A Few Words About Us

Asthika Dharmam is a non-profit organization established with the goal of educating and enlightenting Hindu Dharma through Vedas

We educate the public about Hinduism, speak out about issues affecting Hindus worldwide, and build bridges with institutions and individuals whose work aligns with our objectives and global goals of our organization.
